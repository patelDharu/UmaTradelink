Corporate Social Responsibility

Uma Tradelink is dedicated to creating positive, sustainable change in the communities we serve. We treat corporate social responsibility as a long-term commitment — empowering people through skills training, livelihood support, and environment-friendly initiatives that deliver real impact.

Local Employment

Uma Tradelink strongly believes in empowering local communities by creating employment opportunities. We encourage local talent to apply for open positions and also provide traineeships and internships to students and fresh graduates, helping them gain valuable industry experience.

Upgrading Skills

Our employees are our biggest strength. At Uma Tradelink, we actively promote skill development and continuous learning through both internal and external training programs. We also support employees who pursue executive education courses, ensuring their personal and professional growth.

Environment

We are sincerely committed to the protection of the environment. Strict measures are taken to ensure that our operations do not harm nature in any way. We also promote sustainable practices, including safe handling of chemicals, resource efficiency, and eco-friendly initiatives.

Safety

Safety is at the core of Uma Tradelink’s values. From handling hazardous materials responsibly to ensuring workplace safety for our employees, we follow stringent safety standards. Our CSR focus extends beyond business to create positive and sustainable change, empowering communities and safeguarding future generations.
