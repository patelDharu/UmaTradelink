Welcome To Uma Tradelink

Our story began in 2020 in Lathidad, Botad, Gujarat, with a small yet determined setup that became the foundation of our business operations. From these humble beginnings, Uma Tradelink has grown into a trusted name in the industry, building strong relationships and expanding our presence across India and beyond.

We specialize in basic chemicals, acids, bulk pharma intermediates, and dye intermediates, and proudly serve as authorised distributors for leading chemical brands. With modern infrastructure and all required licenses, we ensure safe handling and storage of hazardous and sensitive chemicals.

To support our operations, we have a fleet of dedicated carriers and delivery vans, ensuring efficient and timely distribution to businesses across regions. At Uma Tradelink, delivering quality, safety, and trust is at the heart of all our activities — a commitment reflected in both our business practices and our social responsibility initiatives.
